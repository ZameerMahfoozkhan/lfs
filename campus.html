Campus & Infrastructure

A conducive environment for holistic learning and growth.

Location & Environment

Little Flower School is situated in a calm and quiet place in Kalyan Bhadarsa, Ayodhya. It is conveniently located 2 km from Kalyan Bhadarsa and 12 km from the Ayodhya District Headquarters, right on the Ayodhya-Sultanpur Road.


The school buildings are housed within a sprawling land area of 6 acres. The campus is secured with boundary walls on all sides, ensuring a safe, neat, clean, and hygienic environment for students.


Our multi-storied buildings are strong, sturdy, and aesthetically designed. All rooms are spacious, well-ventilated, and equipped with modern amenities including:

  • Fans and proper lighting
  • Digital display boards
  • CCTV surveillance
  • Public announcement systems
