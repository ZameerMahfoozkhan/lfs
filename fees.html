Fees Structure

Annual Fees & Payment Schedule

Tuition & Admission Fees

The fee structure is revised annually to meet the growing needs of the institution and provide best-in-class facilities to our students.


Payment Schedule

School fees are generally payable in four quarterly installments (e.g., April, July, October, January).

  • Fees must be paid by the 15th of the due month.
  • A late fee is applicable if the due date is crossed.
  • Fees once paid are non-refundable.

Note: The detailed fee chart (including Admission Fee, Tuition Fee, Exam Fee, Digital Class Fee) is strictly internal and available in the School Prospectus or at the School Office.
