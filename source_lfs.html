Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Faizabad

           Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Faizabad, U.P., is an English Medium High School established in the Catholic Diocese of Allahabad and owned and administered by the "Little Flower Mission Education Society, Gorakhpur", a registered charitable society under the control of CST Fathers, who belong to the "Little Flower Congregation", a Catholic religious set up. The school has been serving the people of Harraya since 2004. It is following the syllabus pattern of ICSE, New Delhi, with 'No Objection Certificate' from the Government of U.P.

           Being governed by the Catholic Church, a minority community, the school has the primary objective of educating the children of the community. However, we shall not spare any effort in rendering our service to all people, irrespective of caste and creed, so as to conserve their religion, language and culture. It is a school for both boys and girls.

          It is situated in a calm and quiet place on the side of Faizabad-sultanpur Main road and is about 12 km off the district headquarters. The school is housed with in a land area of 6 acres. The campus is neat, clean and hygenic. Buildings are strong and sturdy, beautiful and attractive. All rooms are spacious, well ventilated, clean, fitted with fans, lights and visual class faciltiy.

AIM

           The School aims at the integral development of the personality of every child, to make him committed to the service of God and the nation. We instil into them a spirit of compassion, unselfish service, dedication to duty, respect for elders, love for the fellow men, thirst for knowledge and wisdom. It will be our earnest effort to give equal importance to education and the moral development of the students.

          The school emphasises that the students not only excell at their studies but also be an integral part of the society by contributing morals, values and good services to the welfare of humanity.The students are exhorted to march forward forthright with right consciousness.

          Education without decipline will lead to beating on the bush; directionless, wayless and careless actions. Therefore, in the school we inspire and instruct children to be deciplined, well mannered and to be respectable individuals in the society. The students are given the awarness not to confine to the materialistic society and yeild to their shortcomings, rather come up and face the life with bold spirit.

          The students are motivated to instill basic knowledge about rights and responsibilities to govern themselves and the society. They are reminded to keep a close watch to the society and do what is right not yielding to the rough circumstancess in their life.

          Our full concentration is to build up confidence in each and every child to enable him/her to march forward with never failing determination. Thus, we aim at moulding individuals into intellectually and emotionally balanced personalities.

          Students are encouraged to maintain high ideals and strive for excellence in all fields. The important ideas that are inculcated into them are the values of freedom & its judicious use and respect for law and order. They are taught to abide by the moral principles, and unselfishness in the service of their country and fellowmen.
