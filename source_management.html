Ownership and Administration.

          The ownership and management of Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Ayodhya is vested with Little Flower Mission Education Society, Gorakhpur, a registered charitable society. The management and control of L.F.M.E.Society is in the hands of CST Fathers belonging to the Little Flower Province, Gorakhpur of Little Flower Congregation, Thrikkakara, Kerala.

          The local Administration of Little Flower School,Kalyan Bhadarsa, Faizabad is entrusted to the Manager of the school Rev. Fr. Mathew Puthenpura CST and the Principal of the school Rev. Fr. Biju Anjeckaril CST.
