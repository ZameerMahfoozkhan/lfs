Who We Are

A legacy of excellence in education since 2004.

Our History

Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Faizabad, U.P., is an English Medium High School established in the Catholic Diocese of Allahabad.


Learn More About Us

  • Little Flower Mission Education Society
  • Little Flower Province
  • CST Fathers & Founders

The school has been serving the people of Harraya since 2004. It follows the syllabus pattern of ICSE, New Delhi, with a 'No Objection Certificate' from the Government of U.P.


Being governed by the Catholic Church, a minority community, the school has the primary objective of educating the children of the community. However, we spare no effort in rendering our service to all people, irrespective of caste and creed, to conserve their religion, language, and culture.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Aim

The School aims at the integral development of the personality of every child, to make him committed to the service of God and the nation. We instil a spirit of:

  • Compassion
  • Unselfish Service
  • Dedication to Duty
  • Thirst for Knowledge and Wisdom

Our Motto

"Gyanenam Mukti"

Liberation through Knowledge.

We believe that true education liberates the mind and soul, empowering students to face life with a bold spirit.

Student Development

We inspire and instruct children to be disciplined, well-mannered, and respectable individuals. Our full concentration is to build confidence in each child, enabling them to march forward with never-failing determination.
