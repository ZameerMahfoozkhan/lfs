Welcome to Our School

Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Faizabad, U.P., is an English Medium School established in the Catholic Diocese of Allahabad and owned and administered by Little Flower Mission Education Society, Gorakhpur. The school is controlled by the CST Fathers, belonging to the 'Little Flower Congregation'.


Since 2015, we have been serving the people of Kalyan Bhadarsa with a curriculum following the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), New Delhi. We hold a 'No Objection Certificate' from the Government of U.P.
