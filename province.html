Little Flower Province (Gorakhpur-Nepal)

The Gorakhpur-Nepal Little Flower Province is a major administrative unit of the Little Flower Congregation (CST Fathers). It encompasses a vast area of service including the Gorakhpur region in India and extensive missions in Nepal.


History & Mission

The mission in Gorakhpur began in 1970, with a vision to serve the people of North India through education, social work, and spiritual guidance. Over the decades, the province has expanded its footprint significantly.


In 1994, the mission extended to Nepal with the establishment of the Nepal Little Flower Society (NLFS), further broadening the scope of its charitable activities. Today, the province manages numerous schools, technical institutes, and social service centers, touching thousands of lives daily.


Provincial Administration

The province is led by a Provincial Superior and his council, headquartered at Jyoti Bhavan, Gorakhpur. They provide strategic direction and oversight to all institutions under their care, ensuring they remain true to the congregation's motto of service and love.
