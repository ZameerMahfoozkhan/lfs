School Library

A Window to the World of Knowledge

Knowledge Centre

Our library is the heart of academic life at the school. It houses a vast collection of books ranging from academic textbooks to fiction, biographies, encyclopedias, and reference materials.


The peaceful and spacious environment encourages students to develop a habit of reading. Regular subscriptions to newspapers, magazines, and journals ensure that students are aware of current affairs and global developments.
