Location & infrastructure

          Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa,Ayodhya is situated in a calm and quiet place. It is 2 km away from Kalyan Bhadarsa, 12 km away from Ayodhya District Head Quarters and on the side of Ayodhya-Sultanpur Road. The school buildings are housed with in a land area of 6 acres and constructed as multi-storied buildings. The campus is secured with boundry walls on all sides. The campus is neat, clean and hygenic. Buildings are strong and sturdy, beautiful and attractive. All rooms are spacious, well ventilated, clean, fitted with fans, lights, digital display board, CCTV camera, announcement system etc..

           The school has 36594 ft2 (3399 m2) of buildings including class rooms, science labs, computer lab, library, office, staff rooms, staircases, toilets, exam hall, launch, stores and veranda. All the labs are well equiped, well arranged and spacious enough to conduct practicals of one class in one go. Computer lab is arranged with sufficient number of computers. Library has enough reading materials of current affairs and sufficient books for acquiring better knowledge and for entertainment.

           Electricity supply is ensured throughout school hours with the provisions of generators. There is provision for running drinking water supply through pipes in all the floors of the building. There are deep hand pumps inside the campus for drinking water purpose. Every floor of the school building has toilet facilities on either side of the building. The school has one football court, two tennis courts, one volley ball court and facility for table tennis, carrom and chess. For the kindergarten children there are facilities for play and entertainment.The school is also providing school bus service for the needy students.
