Admission Procedure

The academic year of the schools is from April to March. Admission to the L.K.G are taken in March-April. Admissions to other classes will be entertained only at the instance of vacancy in      that class, which shall be determined and announced after the commencement of the new academic session.

Notification regarding admission will be made in advance and the prospectus with Registration Form shall be available from the school office on payment of application fee (not refundable)    nbsp;mentioned in the prospectus during the prescribed hours only.

Student's name will be registered at the time of issue of Prospectus and Registration Form. Only those students whose names are registered and who have submitted the duly filled in     Registration Forms shall be considered for admission.

Registration Does Not Guarantee Admission. It will be subject to Merit and Availability of seats.

The duly filled in Registration Form along with required documents shall be submitted to the school office on the given date(s) only.

Application for admission to classes above V will be entertained only if the pupil is from a recognized English Medium School. In that case, the Transfer Certificate must be countersigned by    a Competent Authority.

Students should have attained the minimum age fixed for admission to a class calculated on April 1 of the year of seeking    admission.

For all admission seekers there will be an interaction of parents and student with principal/teachers and written test for classes UKG and above.

A list of students selected for admission will be published in school Notice Board on the date and time given. Date and time for    admission also will be published along with the selection list.

Students whose names appear in the selection list are eligible for taking admission. The following requirements shall be     completed at the time of taking admission.

1. The parents(s) shall fill up and submitt the Admission Form given to them.

2. The parents(s) shall deposit the first instalment of fees in the School Office at the time of admission.

3. The following Documents shall be submitted at the time of taking admission. Those who fail to submit them will lose their      chance for admission.

(a) Original Copy of certificate proving Date of Birth as mentioned in Clause A: Age of the prospectus.

OR

    Xerox copy of the above duly attested by a first class Magistrate. [For Classes LKG to V only]

b) Original Copy of Transfer Certificate as mentioned in "Admission" in the prospectus [For Classes UKG & above only]. The      original copy of the T.C. shall be countersinged for classes VI and above.

(c) Mark Sheet [Report Card] from the school last studied.
