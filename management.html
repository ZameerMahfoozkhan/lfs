Ownership & Management

The ownership and management of Little Flower School, Kalyan Bhadarsa, Ayodhya is vested with the Little Flower Mission Education Society, Gorakhpur, a registered charitable society.


The management and control of L.F.M.E. Society is in the hands of CST Fathers belonging to the Little Flower Province, Gorakhpur of the Little Flower Congregation, Thrikkakara, Kerala.
